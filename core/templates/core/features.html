Features and Help Menu

Welcome to MG Tourism interactive help menu!

First Steps

Click here to register. We guarantee you that your data will never be misused and is securely stored.

Click here to login. After logging in, you can access your profile.

You can edit your profile by clicking here.

Things

You can see all the things here. There, you can filter by many attributes, but if you are looking for a specific thing simply use the search functionality in the navigation bar.

After logging in, you can contribute to MG Tourism by adding your own things. Click on "Create a Thing" on the navigation bar to begin.

With the right permissions, you can edit and delete already existing things.

Comments

After logging in, you can go on the detail page of a thing and leave a comment. You can only leave one comment per thing. After leaving a comment, the thing you commented on will be visible under the "Visited" tab in your profile.

Remember that you cannot comment twice on the same thing, instead edit your comment. To edit your comment, visit the detail page of the thing you changed your mind about or go to your profile under the "Visited" tab and find it there. Locate your comment and click the "Edit" button.

Follow the instructions for editing a comment. In the editing page you will see an option to delete the comment. Confirm the deletion before the action is completed.

Plans

After logging in, you can create a plan. You can create a plan within your profile or in the detail page of a thing by pressing the "Add to Plan" button. Plans can be public or private. Public plans are visible to all other users and can be favorited by them.

After creating a plan, visit the detail page of a thing, click the "Add to Plan" button, and select a plan. The things of a plan are visible under the "My Plans" tab in your profile.

If you decided something should not be in your plan anymore, go to the "My Plans" tab in your profile, find the respective plan and remove the unwanted thing.

To favorite a plan, visit someone's profile, go under their plans tab and click the favorite button. You can only see and favorite plans that are public, and after favoriting a plan, it will be visible under your "Favorited Plans" tab in your profile.

You can unfavorite a plan by going to the "Favorited Plans" tab of your profile and pressing the "Unfavorite" button.
